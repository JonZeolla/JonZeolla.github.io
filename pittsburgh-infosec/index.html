Welcome to my guide to the Pittsburgh InfoSec community

There are a lot of different groups in Pittsburgh, but all of them have a different cadence, focus, and composition. This guide is an attempt to help people find the right group for them.

Please note that all of the below information is specific to Pittsburgh and based on my understanding of the events.


User Groups

User groups are groups that meet more than once a year, cover a variety of topics, and are often attended by less than one hundred people per event.
